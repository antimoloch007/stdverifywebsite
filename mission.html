Our Mission

STD Verify: A Social Purpose Corporation

At STD Verify SPC, we are on a mission to revolutionize sexual health verification within the LGBTQ+ community. As a Social Purpose Corporation, we balance our commitment to creating shareholder value with our dedication to serving a broader social purpose.

Our Vision

We envision a world where discussing STD status is destigmatized, where sexual health is prioritized, and where individuals can make informed decisions about their intimate relationships with confidence and dignity.

Our Social Purpose

STD Verify is committed to:

  • Reducing STD Transmission: By providing a secure and anonymous way to verify STD status, we aim to contribute to the reduction of STD transmission rates within the LGBTQ+ community.
  • Destigmatizing Sexual Health: We are working to normalize conversations about sexual health and STD status, removing the shame and stigma that often surround these discussions.
  • Empowering Informed Consent: Our platform enables individuals to make truly informed decisions about their sexual health and the risks they are willing to accept.
  • Protecting Privacy: Through our innovative zero-knowledge proof technology, we verify health information without storing or exposing sensitive medical data, ensuring user privacy is paramount.

Why a Social Purpose Corporation?

We chose to incorporate as a Social Purpose Corporation because we believe that business can be a powerful force for social good. This legal structure allows us to:

  • Balance profit motives with our mission to improve public health outcomes
  • Make decisions that prioritize our social impact even when they may not maximize short-term profits
  • Build trust with our community by legally committing to our social purpose
  • Attract investors, employees, and users who share our values and vision

Our Commitment to the LGBTQ+ Community

STD Verify was founded within the LGBTQ+ community to address specific healthcare challenges faced by our community. We recognize the historical barriers to healthcare access and the disproportionate impact of STDs on LGBTQ+ individuals. Our platform is designed with these challenges in mind, creating a solution that is respectful, inclusive, and empowering.
